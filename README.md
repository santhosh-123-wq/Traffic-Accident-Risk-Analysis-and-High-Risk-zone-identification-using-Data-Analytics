# Traffic-Accident-Risk-Analysis-and-High-Risk-zone-identification-using-Data-Analytics
Project description: Traffic Accident Analysis

This project focuses on identifying accident-prone zones and analyzing traffic accident patterns using a data-driven approach. Historical traffic data, including accident reports, road conditions, traffic density, and signal timings, is analyzed to uncover patterns contributing to accidents.

The study aims to develop insights that help predict high-risk areas enable proactive safety measures. By leveraging data analytics, the project supports traffic authorities in reducing accident frequency and improving overall road safety.

Business Objectives:

The primary business objective of this analysis is to understand how traffic conditions, road infrastructure, and environmental factors contribute to accident risks, enabling the City Traffic Department to:

- Improve road safety by identifying high-risk zones
- Reduce the number of accidents at intersections and highways
- Enhance traffic signal optimization and road condition monitoring
- Support data-driven decision-making for traffic management
- Enable proactive accident prevention strategies
